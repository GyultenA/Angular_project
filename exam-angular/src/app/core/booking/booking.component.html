Book for a day

There are two to three play groups during our pet daycare: small dog play group (includes small breed dogs and small pups), medium play group (if our daycare is large enough to be spilt into 3 groups; includes high energy small dogs and puppies, and calm-tempered medium/larger breed dogs), and big dog play group (includes large breed dogs and high energy larger breed puppies). Group placement for dogs will be determined by the staff based on temperament assessments and the other individuals in that day’s groups.
